# FMoSE

**Future Mobility Operation Laboratory** & **Mobility Cybersecurity Lab**

---

## About

A joint research group at **Kookmin University**, established through a strategic collaboration between two specialized laboratories:

- **[FMO Lab](https://fmo.kookmin.ac.kr)** — Future Mobility Operation Laboratory
- **[MoSE Lab](https://mose.kookmin.ac.kr)** — Mobility Cybersecurity Lab

Our current research focuses on **UAM (Urban Air Mobility) Cybersecurity**.
